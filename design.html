Design

Print | Design

My job at Ball State requires me to surprisingly multifaceted. Over the years, I have done quite a variety of print work, from magazine ads to university mailers, posters and banners. It’s always enjoyable to break out and do something a bit different.

Identity | Design

Along with video graphics, identity work is probably the side of design I dabble in the least. Even so, I have been responsible for creating and maintaining the branding on a number of projects such as The Digital Corps—a student staffed design, development and video studio— and Innovate WithIN–a joint project with the Indiana Department of Education and the IEDC, to create a statewide entrepreneurship contest for high school students.

Web / UI | Design

Web design and HTML/CSS are a passion that I don’t get to indulge in as much as I would like. Often I am overseeing web projects, working to ensure proper and efficient UX/UI standards and trends, and it’s always a joy when I get to roll up my sleeves and jump into those kind of projects myself. Or when I get to design and build one-off projects, such as this portfolio site. I also enjoy redesigning UI/UX experiences as a constructive exercise, like EA’s Anthem game menus below.
