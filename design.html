Design

Print | Design

While I primarily consider myself a web designer, my job at Ball State requires me to surprisingly multifaceted. Over the years, I have done quite a variety of print work, from magazine ads to university mailers, posters and banners. It’s always enjoyable to break out and do something a bit different.

Identity | Design

Along with video graphics, identity work is probably the side of design I dabble in the least. Even so, I have been responsible for creating and maintaining the branding on a number of projects such as The Digital Corps—a student staffed design, development and video studio— and Innovate WithIN–a joint project with the Indiana Department of Education and the IEDC, to create a statewide entrepreneurship contest for high school students.

Web / UI | Design

Formed in partnership between Ball State University, the Indiana Department of Education and the Indiana Economic Development Council, Innovate WithIN is a high school entrepreneurship pitch competition. I created the initial logo and branding, before transitioning to project art director and designer.
