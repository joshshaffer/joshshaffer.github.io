What is this?
This is an independent and original Batman story.
Who made this?
This entire thing is a one man show. I wrote, drew, colored and lettered the comic, along with designing and coding the website.
Is this an official Batman story?
Nope. This work is in no way associated with or authorized by either DC Entertainment or Warner Bros.
Are you making any money off of this?
Not a dime. You may have noticed a distinct lack of advertising on this site, and this comic will never be repackaged and printed. (So please don't sue me, Warner Bros!)
What are you trying to accomplish with this?
Simply put, I had a Batman story that I wanted to tell. My day job is a graphic designer/front end web designer, and I was looking for additional side artistic outlets. This is just for fun.
How often is this updated?
New pages should be posted every Monday and Thursday.
