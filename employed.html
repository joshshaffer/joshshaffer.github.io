HOW TO BE EMPLOYED

Print

I illustrated and designed the cover along with chapter illustrations for "How to be Employed when you Graduate." This project was a lot of fun. Each chapter began with a single page comic related to the topic at hand. The idea was to help make the subject matter instantly relatable to collage age kids. I also created additional art for the accompanying iOS app. I don't often get to work with print, so it's always a fun experience.
