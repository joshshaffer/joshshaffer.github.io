AVENGERS

Illustration

A personal project simply born out of enthusiasm for the Avengers films. The figures were all individually drawn digitally and then imported into Illustrator and vectorized. This allowed me to have complete control over the layout while still maintaining the energy of something being hand drawn. The image was designed with the intention of being eventually screen printed, so I attempted to capture the distressed look of the countless pop culture shirts I wore as a child, while still remaining dynamic and modern.
