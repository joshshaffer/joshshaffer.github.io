About Me
I am at heart a storyteller. I live to create, whether it is through cartoons, illustrations, web, app or print design. My job with any project is to convey emotion and information to the viewer. My primary background is illustration, but I continue to work to expand my skillsets to cover as much as possible. Put a cup of coffee in my hand and I am just as happy writing HTML and CSS as I am working in Illustrator or producing digital or handdrawn illustrations.
