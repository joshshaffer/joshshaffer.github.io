Ball State University

As part of my job at Ball State I have created a wide variety of both internal and public facing print and digital pieces, ranging from print advertisements, promotional banners and signs to video titles and lower thirds and even websites and native apps, all while maintaining and working within the University branding.

Digital Corps

A unique part of the Information Technology department of Ball State University, The Digital Corps exists as a digital agency, providing custom video and web development for both internal projects and external state partnerships. Part of my job responsibilities include managing internal design work for The Digital Corps, as well as design training and leading intrustructional workshops for undergraduate and postgraduate student employees.
